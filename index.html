When & Where

The CtrlShift group has weekly meetings every Thursday from 5pm to 8pm. Can't make it at 5pm, can't stay till 8pm? No problem! Everyone is welcome to come and go as they please. Please be sure to check the meeting location shown in the calendar as meeting locations can change week to week. We hope to see you soon!
